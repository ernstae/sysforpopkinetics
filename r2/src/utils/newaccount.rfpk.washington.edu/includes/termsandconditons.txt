Purpose of Web Site

This Web site is designed to disseminate information developed through support of the National Institutes of Health and other public and private sources, and is managed by Dr. Paolo Vicini and the Resource Facility for Population Kinetics (RFPK), who can be contacted through http://www.rfpk.washington.edu. This Web site serves the University of Washington's mission to disseminate knowledge in the public interest and serves the RFPK mission to provide population kinetic analysis services to the scientific community. This information is provided on the following terms and conditions.

The Web site provides simulation and estimation of mixed effects models through the System for Population Kinetics web service (hereafter the "SPK Service"). The site is subject to change without notice.
