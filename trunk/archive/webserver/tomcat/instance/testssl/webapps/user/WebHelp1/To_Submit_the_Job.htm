To Submit the Job:

Click "Submit Job". The "Job Submission Dialog" opens. The dialog has three tabs: Model, Dataset and Job.

The Model tab describes the model you have chosen to analyze the data. A model name is needed. A description and a log are optional.

 

 

The Dataset tab describes the dataset, and it also has a log and a description.

 

 

The Job tab specifies the method of analysis and has space for a job abstract.

 

 

When you click on OK, you should see something like this window:

 

 

Your job has now been submitted.

 

Click "My Jobs". The Job List opens. Find the current job on the job list and check its status under "Status Code." Soon, it should read "Successful Run."

 

Click on the job entry to open the "Job Information" page for that job.

 

 

Click "Resuls" to access the summary report.  "OK" the following informational pop-up and the Summary Report finally appears in the viewing pane. Scrolling to the bottom provides a view of the final parameter values for THETA, OMEGA and SIGMA as well as standard errors and 95% confidence intervals:
