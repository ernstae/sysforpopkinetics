This Web site is designed to disseminate information developed through support of the National Institutes of Health and other public and private sources, and is managed by Dr. Paolo Vicini and the Resource Facility for Population Kinetics (RFPK), who can be contacted through http://www.rfpk.washington.edu. The Web site provides simulation and estimation of mixed effects models through the System for Population Kinetics web service (hereafter the "SPK Service"). The site is subject to change without notice.
