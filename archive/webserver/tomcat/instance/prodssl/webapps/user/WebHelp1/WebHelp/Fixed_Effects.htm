6. Fixed Effects

In this screen, enter the initial estimates and bounds for the fixed effects parameter THETA. A glance at the data for this simple example might produce an initial estimate of 72.

 

  • Enter 72 in the Initial Estimate box and
  • Click Enter on your keyboard to get the default bounds (7.2, 720).
  • Click "Add" to enter these estimates and the triple (7.2, 72, 720) will appear in the List window.

 

 

Click Next> to continue.

 

(In a more complex example where there are multiple THETAs, add the initial estimate for each, THETA individually.)

 

(Note: setting the bounds too large may produce poor results since the bounds are used in setting the parameter optimization stopping criteria.)
