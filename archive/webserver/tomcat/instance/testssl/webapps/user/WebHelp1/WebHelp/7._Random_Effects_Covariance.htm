7. Random Effects Covariance

In this screen, enter the initial estimate for the population variance term OMEGA. A reasonable overestimate of the standard deviation is 30, so use an initial estimate of 900 for the variance term OMEGA.

 

  • Check "New Block" and "Diagonal" ("Full Matrix" would be OK too since the matrix is 1x1).
  • In the "Block Size" menu, select "1" (since we only have a single variance term) and click "Enter data". The "Diagonal Matrix Entry Form" will pop open.
  • Enter the estimated variance 900.
  • Click "Add". The entry form will close and $OMEGA DIAGONAL(1) 900 will appear in the window below.

 

 

Click Next> to continue.

 

(In a more complex example where the covariance matrix OMEGA has higher dimension, select the matrix dimension in the "Block Size" menu and click "Enter data" to get the entry form for filling in variance/covariance initial estimates.)
